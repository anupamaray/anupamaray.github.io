My Research Interests

I am a Researcher in the department of AI for interaction where I work with multimodal data to build solutions to improve technical support services and improve Watson products. I have worked on building deep parsing for extraction of attributes from text for improving search and also build text classifiers for automating customer support services. I am developing novel multimodal meta-learning algorithm for depression severity prediction using audio, video and textual data. I am also working on building full NLP stack for Indian languages.
